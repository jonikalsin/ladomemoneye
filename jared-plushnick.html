    Plushnick began his career at AccuWeather as a broadcast meteorologist. He then moved to Bangor, Maine, and joined WABI TV 5 as a meteorologist. In August 2011, he was appointed the new chief meteorologist at KAGS HD. Jared then transferred to KVUE in October 204 where he worked as a meteorologist until April 2017.
