Jane Levy - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts, Wiki

Table of Biography

Jane Levy is a well-known American actress. Jane Levy is best known for her appearance as Tessa Altman on the American sitcom television series “Suburgatory” from 2011 to 2014. Similarly, she has appeared in several famous TV shows and films, including ‘Fun Size,’ ‘Nobody Walks,’ ‘The Evil Dead,’ ‘Don’t Breathe,’ among others.

Early Life and Childhood

Jane Levy was born on December 29, 1989, in Los Angeles County, California, in the United States. Jane Colburn Levy is her given name. As of the year 2021, her age will be 32 years old. Similarly, she was born to a mediator’s father, Lester Levy, and an artist and florist’s mother, Mary Tilbury.

She grew up in the Northern California city of San Anselmo. She holds an American nationality. Her father’s ancestors are Ashkenazi Jews, and her mother’s ancestors are English, Irish, and Scottish. Capricorn is her astrological sign.

Moreover, in terms of college, she attended Sir Francis Drake High School, where she was a member of the hip-hop dance team as well as the soccer team’s captain. She then enrolled for a year at Goucher College in Baltimore, Maryland. She then moved to New York City’s Stella Adler Studio of Acting and graduated from the Conservatory.

Professional Life

In early 2011, Jane Levy played Mandy Milkovich in the first season of Showtime’s comedy-drama ‘Shameless.’ The next year, she landed her first leading appearance as ‘Tessa Altman’ on the American sitcom television series ‘Suburgatory.’ She starred in two films in 2012, ‘Fun Size’ and ‘Nobody Walks.’

Similarly, in 2013, she played the drug-addicted Mia Allen in the remake of the horror movie film ‘The Evil Dead.’ She appeared in two feature films in 2014, ‘About Alex’ and ‘Bang Bang Baby.’ She starred in two short films in 2015, ‘Nicholas & Hillary’ and ‘Here Now.’ The same year, she starred as Kate in the comedy film ‘Frank and Cindy.’

She appeared in the horror film ‘Don’t Breathe’ in 2016. In the same year, she starred in the action-comedy film ‘Monster Trucks.’ In 2017, Levy starred in the comedy horror film ‘I Don’t Feel at Home in This World Anymore’ as well as the Showtime series ‘Twin Peaks.’ In 2018, she starred as the female lead in the romantic thriller film ‘Pretenders.’

In the same year, she starred in Sony Crackle’s zombie comedy film ‘Office Uprising.’ She was cast as the lead in the Netflix drama series ‘What/If’ in 2018. She played Zoey Clarke in the NBC musical drama ‘Zoey’s Extraordinary Playlist,’ which debuted on January 7, 2020, on NBC.
