The Top 10 Greatest Boston Celtics of All-Time

The Boston Celtics, one of the most storied franchises in NBA history, have had a long and successful run in the league. Over the years, the team has been home to some of the greatest basketball players to ever grace the court. From legendary coaches to iconic players, the Celtics have left an indelible mark on the sport. In this article, we will take a closer look at the top 10 greatest Boston Celtics of all time.

1. Bill Russell

Bill Russell is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. During his 13-year career with the Celtics, he won an incredible 11 NBA championships, making him the most successful player in NBA history. Russell was known for his exceptional defense, shot-blocking abilities, and leadership skills. He was a dominant force on the court and was instrumental in establishing the Celtics dynasty.

2. Larry Bird

Larry Bird, often referred to as "Larry Legend," is considered one of the greatest forwards to ever play the game. Bird, a 12-time NBA All-Star, spent his entire 13-year career with the Celtics. He was a versatile player known for his scoring ability, basketball IQ, and clutch performances. Bird won three NBA championships with the Celtics and was named NBA Finals MVP twice.

3. Paul Pierce

Paul Pierce, also known as "The Truth," had a remarkable 15-year career with the Celtics. He played a pivotal role in leading the team to their 17th NBA championship in 2008. Pierce was a skilled scorer and a clutch performer. He ranks second on the Celtics' all-time scoring list and was a 10-time NBA All-Star.

4. John Havlicek

John Havlicek was a key member of the Celtics' dynasty during the 1960s and 70s. He played his entire 16-year career with the team and won eight NBA championships. Havlicek was a versatile player who could score, rebound, and play exceptional defense. He was known for his incredible endurance and relentless work ethic.

5. Bob Cousy

Bob Cousy, often referred to as "The Houdini of the Hardwood," was a pioneer of modern point guard play. He played for the Celtics from 1950 to 1963 and won six NBA championships. Cousy was known for his exceptional ball-handling skills, passing ability, and court vision. He revolutionized the position and paved the way for future point guards.

6. Kevin McHale

Kevin McHale was a dominant force in the paint during his 13-year career with the Celtics. He won three NBA championships with the team and was named NBA Finals MVP in 1984. McHale was known for his scoring ability, footwork, and post moves. He formed a formidable frontcourt duo with Larry Bird and helped the Celtics become one of the most successful teams of the 1980s.

7. Sam Jones

Sam Jones is often overlooked when discussing the greatest Celtics players, but his contributions to the team cannot be understated. Jones won an incredible 10 NBA championships during his 12-year career with the Celtics. He was known for his scoring ability and clutch performances in the playoffs. Jones ranks third on the Celtics' all-time scoring list.

8. Bill Sharman

Bill Sharman was a key player for the Celtics during the 1950s. He played for the team from 1951 to 1961 and won four NBA championships. Sharman was a deadly shooter and a tenacious defender. He was named an NBA All-Star eight times and was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame as both a player and a coach.

9. Robert Parish

Robert Parish, also known as "The Chief," was a vital part of the Celtics' success in the 1980s. He won three NBA championships with the team and was known for his durability and consistent play. Parish ranks second on the Celtics' all-time rebounding and shot-blocking lists. He was named an NBA All-Star nine times during his career.

10. Tom Heinsohn

Tom Heinsohn was a key player for the Celtics during the 1950s and 60s. He won eight NBA championships with the team and was known for his scoring ability and tenacious defense. Heinsohn was named an NBA All-Star six times and went on to have a successful coaching career with the Celtics, winning two additional championships.

The Boston Celtics have had a rich history filled with some of the greatest basketball players of all time. From Bill Russell to Larry Bird, these players have left an indelible mark on the franchise and the sport as a whole. Their contributions to the team's success and their impact on the game will forever be remembered.
