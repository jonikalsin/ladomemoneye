Justin Thomas – Professional Life

Justin Thomas is a professional golfer. He was a member of the University of Alabama golf team, where he won six times as a member of the Crimson Tide. Likewise, he began his professional career in 2013 and qualified for the Web.com Tour through qualifying school.

At the 2014 Nationwide Children’s Hospital Championship, he won his first professional event.

Similarly, the golfer has seven top-10 finishes and 15 top-25 finishes in 2015. He also has a fourth-place finish at the Quicken Loans National and Sanderson Farms Championship being his best.

In addition, he won his first PGA Tour event, the CIMB Classic, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, on November 1, 2015, by a single shot over Adam Scott.
