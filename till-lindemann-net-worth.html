Till Lindemann’s Net Worth

Till Lindemann is a German musician, composer, and poet with a net worth of $8 million. He founded the popular heavy metal/goth band Rammstein in 1994, known for their over-the-top performances involving fire and simulated sex acts. Lindemann actually earned his pyrotechnic license due to past injuries and damages caused by the use of fire in their shows.

Before beginning his music career as a drummer, Lindemann attended a sports school and competed in the European Junior Swimming Championships, being invited to the Olympics before an injury ended his swimming career. He worked various odd jobs before writing his own music, and has also served as a guest performer for other artists and published two books of poetry.
