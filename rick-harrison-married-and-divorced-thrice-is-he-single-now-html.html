Rick Harrison Married and Divorced Thrice Is He Single Now?

History channel is home to many unique shows like The Curse of Oak Island, The UnXplained, but when the relatively niche Pawn Stars premiered, no one imagined it would become an enormous hit. 

The popular reality series is set in the world-famous Gold & Silver Pawn Shop on the outskirts of Las Vegas and is all about showcasing incredible artifacts and people eager to sell their stuff and at their desired price. 

But of course, these people first have to go through the Gold & Silver employees, with the most popular one being Rick Harrison. Known as “The Spotter,” by the other cast members, he is the current owner of the pawnshop. 

While many people are familiar with his work because of the show, his personal life, especially his love life, is not always highlighted. For fans wondering “Who is Rick Harrison married to,” here is a breakdown of Harrison’s love life. 

Rick Harrison’s Third Wife

In July 2013, Harrison tied the knot for the third time with Deanna Burditt, who was also marrying for the third time in Laguna Beach. 

The couple had gotten engaged in 2012 on Valentine’s Day. 

According to People, the couple exchanged their vows traditionally in front of 180 guests, but there were also fun, non-traditional elements in their wedding. First off, his co-star Chumlee was the ring bearer, and Counting Cars star Danny Koker officiated as the minister.

Following the nuptials, a rock band called Steel Panther entertained the guests, after which they even went home with party favors, which was a bobblehead of the duo in their wedding attire.

But on October 16, 2021, TMZ reported the couple had quietly divorced a year ago. 

According to legal documents, Harrison filed for divorce on July 1, 2020, almost seven years after their wedding. In the papers, Harisson stated that the husband and wife had grown apart during their marriage because their “tastes, mental dispositions, views, likes, and dislikes [had] become so divergent.”

This made their marriage incompatible, and there was no hope for reconciliation. 

His wife initially asked for alimony, but according to the documents, the former couple settled on an agreement in September 2020 after finalizing all critical issues. 

Rick Harrison’s Previous Marriages

Before Burditt, Harrison was married twice. 

He was first married to Kim Harrison from 1982 until 1985, and they share sons Corey, 37, and Adam, 36. Corey helps Harrison run their pawnshop and is one of the stars in the History channel series. 

After that, he married Tracy Harrison, and their union lasted over 20 years until their divorce in 2011, the same year he met his third wife, whom he married two years later. With Tracy, Harrison shares son Jake. 

Harrison is also a stepfather to Burditt’s three daughters from her previous marriages. 

As of right now, Harrison appears to be single, but no one knows for sure as he could keep his relationship on the down-low if he has one.
