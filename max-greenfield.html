Max Greenfield is a prominent American actor. Max Greenfield gained fame for his work on films, sitcoms, and series like Cats & Dogs 3: Paws Unite!, The Glass Castle, The Oath, Ice Age: Collision Course, Ugly Betty, The Neighborhood, etc.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Max Greenfield is a popular actor who had recurring roles in Veronica Mars and Ugly Betty. Max is also recognized for co-starring in the WB series Modern Men. He is also the actor who co-starred in the role of Schmidt in New Girl.

For his role in New Girl, Max received nominations for several awards including Emmy, Critics’ Choice Television, and Golden Globe awards.

Further, Max has portrayed the role of Dave Johnson in The Neighborhood since 2018. Similarly, the actor lent his voice to the character Roger in the Ice Age franchise.

Max has also worked as a voice actor for various characters in the shows Bob’s Burgers, Robot Chicken, and BoJack Horseman.

Talking about the beginning, Max began pursuing his career as an actor in 1998. This was right after his high school graduation. Max made guest appearances on dramas like Boston Public, Gilmore Girls, and The O.C.

The actor then received his 1st lead role in Modern Men. In this series, Max played the role of one of three bachelors who hire a life coach to help them understand women.
