What is Tom Sandoval’s Net Worth and Salary?

Tom Sandoval is a successful American model, entrepreneur, and reality television star, with a net worth of $4 million. He is best known for his role on Bravo’s Vanderpump Rules, where he was one of the original cast members.

Sandoval has also dabbled in acting, appearing in films such as “Playing with Fire,” “The Pit and the Pendulum,” and “Puppet Master: Axis of Evil.” Additionally, he leads the cover band Tom Sandoval & the Most Extras.

Vanderpump Rules chronicles the lives of Los Angeles waiters and waitresses at the restaurant SUR, owned by Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Lisa Vanderpump. Sandoval worked as a bartender at SUR for over ten years and famously dated cast member Kristen Doute before moving on to a relationship with Ariana Madix, another cast member, from 2014 until 2023.

In recent seasons, Sandoval has been featured as he opened a restaurant with his best friend and fellow cast member Tom Schwartz. The grand opening of their establishment, TomTom Bar, was even filmed as part of a Vanderpump episode that aired in February 2019.

Early Life

Tom Sandoval was born on July 7, 1983 in St. Louis, Missouri. His parents are Terri and Anthony, and he has a brother named Brian.

Sandoval’s Rise to Fame on “Vanderpump Rules”

Sandoval gained fame as part of the cast of the Bravo reality TV series “Vanderpump Rules,” a spinoff of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.” The show follows the staff of Lisa Vanderpump’s restaurants and bars in the Los Angeles area. Sandoval, who tended bar at SUR, was an original cast member alongside Kristen Doute, Katie Maloney, Jax Taylor, and Stassi Schroeder. The show was a hit and spawned two spinoffs: “Vanderpump Rules After Show” and “Vanderpump Rules: Jax and Brittany Take Kentucky.”

Sandoval was shown launching TomTom, a West Hollywood bar, with Vanderpump and costar Tom Schwartz in 2018. He and Schwartz also opened Schwartz & Sandy’s, a Hollywood bar, which was featured heavily in the show’s tenth season.

Sandoval’s Acting Career

Tom Sandoval made his feature film debut in David DeCoteau’s 2008 film “Playing with Fire.” He went on to appear in several other films including “Reflection,” “Alien Presence,” and DeCoteau’s “The Pit and the Pendulum.” In 2010, he appeared in “Puppet Master: Axis of Evil,” the ninth official entry in the “Puppet Master” series. Sandoval’s other acting credits include “Behind Your Eyes,” “23 Minutes to Sunrise,” “Tango Amargo,” and “The Raiven Destiny.” He has also appeared in “The Wrong Real Estate Agent” and “Scare Us.”

Tom Sandoval & the Most Extras

Tom Sandoval is the lead singer of the cover band Tom Sandoval & the Most Extras, which he co-founded with drummer Jason Bader. The band also includes saxophonist Colin Deas, lead guitarist Kevin Stiphen, bassist Randon Davitt, trumpeter Macrae Eckelberry, rhythm guitarist Michael Aaron, and backing vocalist Maddi Fraser.

Tom Sandoval & the Most Extras tour around the United States, playing cover songs to audiences.

Tom Sandoval’s Cheating Scandal

In 2013, “Vanderpump Rules” star Tom Sandoval entered into a long-term romantic relationship with co-star Ariana Madix. However, the relationship came to a bitter end in 2023 when Sandoval cheated on Madix with their fellow cast member Raquel Leviss.

During an appearance on Howie Mandel’s “The H3 Podcast,” Sandoval admitted to starting the affair with Leviss due to his relationship with Madix becoming stale. The scandal soon became known as “Scandoval” among gossip circles.

Tom Sandoval Buys Valley Village Home for $2.07 Million

Reality TV star Tom Sandoval has purchased a house in the Los Angeles suburb of Valley Village for $2.07 million in February 2019.

The house is expected to be a new addition to Sandoval’s growing real estate portfolio, which includes several properties in California.
