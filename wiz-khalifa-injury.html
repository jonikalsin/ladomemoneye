Wiz Khalifa Injury: What happened to Wiz Khalifa?

Wiz Khalifa injury is a term that refers to how the rap star suffered a ‘small rip in the cartilage’ of his pelvis.

The rapper, whose real name is Cameron Jibril Thomaz, revealed his injury on Twitter on June 25, 2023, saying that it ‘fuckin hurts and pain medicine ain’t helpin’.

Here are some of the details and facts about Wiz Khalifa injury and how he is recovering from it.

Who is Wiz Khalifa?

Wiz Khalifa, a talented rapper, singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur hailing from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, has made a significant impact in the music industry.

Renowned for his chart-topping tracks like “Black and Yellow,” “See You Again,” “We Dem Boyz,” and “Young, Wild & Free,” Wiz Khalifa has captivated audiences with his unique style and lyrical prowess.

In his illustrious career, Wiz Khalifa has graced the music world with an array of albums and mixtapes, including Rolling Papers, O.N.I.F.C., Blacc Hollywood, and Rolling Papers 2. As a visionary, he also established his very own record label, Taylor Gang Entertainment, where he serves as the passionate CEO.

Under Taylor Gang Entertainment, talented artists like Ty Dolla Sign, Juicy J, Chevy Woods, and many others have found a home to showcase their musical talents.

Beyond his music endeavors, Wiz Khalifa is known for his dedication to martial arts and fitness.

He has embraced the disciplines of Muay Thai and Brazilian jiu-jitsu, honing his skills and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Demonstrating his versatility, Wiz Khalifa has collaborated with various athletes and celebrities, enriching his videos and podcasts with their dynamic presence.

Aside from his professional achievements, Wiz Khalifa cherishes his role as a loving and supportive father to his two children.

His deep affection for his family shines through, endearing him to his devoted fan base. With his magnetic personality and undeniable influence on the rap scene, Wiz Khalifa has solidified his status as an influential figure in the music industry.

How did Wiz Khalifa get injured?

Wiz Khalifa got injured by sustaining a ‘small rip in the cartilage’ of his pelvis.

He did not disclose how or when he got injured, but some fans have speculated that it could have been related to his martial arts training or his performance at a concert.

Wiz Khalifa shared his injury on Twitter on June 25, 2023, saying:

“Small rip in the cartilage of my pelvis but I’ll be right back.”

He also shared two videos of him walking with the assistance of two canes and wearing kinesiology tape on his pelvis.

Wiz Khalifa said that he was ‘watchin movies till this swelling go down’ and that he would have ‘super hips after this’.

He later updated his fans by sharing two more videos of him walking without assistance and saying that he liked two legs better than four.

How is Wiz Khalifa recovering from his injury?

Wiz Khalifa is recovering from his injury by resting and taking care of himself.

He has not revealed any details about his treatment or recovery plan, but he has thanked his fans for their support and well wishes.
